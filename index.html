Off the Rack (2026)

Riely didn't just design clothes. She redesigned her life.

Release Date: April 20th, 2026

About the Movie

Riely Thames, a 26-year-old fashion graduate in New Jersey, works for a well-known women's clothing brand where her ideas are repeatedly dismissed by executives. Though talented, constant criticism chips away at her confidence, leaving her stuck running errands instead of designing. Fed up, she quits and launches her own inclusive fashion line. As her debut runway approaches, her former company sabotages her by booking her models and leaking her designs to competitors. With time running out and her reputation on the line, Riely rebuilds her collection from the start, assembling a new team and refining her vision. On the night of the runway, she steps forward, not just as a designer, but as the brand itself, unaware of how far this moment is about to take her.

Cast

  • Riely Thames - A creative 26-year-old fashion graduate and former employee at a popular women's clothing brand.
  • Marcus Linton - Riely's former boss at the fashion company, who repeatedly dismisses her ideas and underestimates her talent.
  • Elena Ortiz - A skilled local tailor who joins Riely's new team and helps rebuild the collection from scratch.
  • Jasper Cole - Riely's supportive friend who's in the fashion industry. He provides advice and encouragement as she launches her own runway show.
